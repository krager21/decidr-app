# Privacy Policy for Decidr

**Last updated: February 2025**

## Overview

Decidr ("the App") is a decision-making app that helps you choose activities through an interactive spinning wheel. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Preferences**: Your activity preferences (indoor/outdoor, mood, energy level, time of day) are stored locally on your device
- **Activity History**: Records of activities you've completed are stored locally on your device
- **Favorites**: Your favorite activities are stored locally on your device

### What We Don't Collect
- We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect usage analytics
- We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to external servers. If you uninstall the App, all data is deleted.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no advertising, in-app purchases, or external links.
